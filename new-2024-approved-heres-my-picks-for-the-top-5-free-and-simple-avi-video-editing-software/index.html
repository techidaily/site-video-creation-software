Advantages: Blender’s price and its powerful features make it an interesting choice as your primary video editing software.

Advantages: Solves all your basic video editing problems, and lets you have fun while editing videos. Avidemux also has solid encoding options and its interface is user-friendly.

Limitations: There are no video effects or transitions, which narrows down the software’s video editing options.
