Step 5: Trimming Your Video in Filmora

Remember that cutting and trimming in Filmora are two different things. Trimming allows you to remove a particular video part from visibility without deleting the portion.

So feel free to skip this step if youre happy learning the cutting technique. But if youre interested in trimming, heres how you do it.

Take your mouse cursor at the beginning or end of the clip you want to trim. Keep holding the clips corner and slide the mouse back or front to trim the part
